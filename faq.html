Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are natural remedies?
Natural remedies refer to alternative approaches to healing and promoting well-being using ingredients and methods found in nature, such as herbs, plants, and holistic practices.
2. How do natural remedies differ from conventional medicine?
Natural remedies often focus on harnessing the healing properties of plants and natural substances, emphasizing prevention and overall wellness. Conventional medicine relies on pharmaceuticals and medical interventions.
